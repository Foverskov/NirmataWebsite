ABOUT NIRMATA [cite: 2]

NIRMATA IS A NEW MODERN ROCK PROJECT BASED IN COPENHAGEN, DENMARK BLENDING POWERFUL VOCALS WITH CAPTIVATING, MELODIC RIFFS AND THUNDEROUS DRUMS. [cite: 2]

WITH A DYNAMIC SOUND THAT COVERS BOTH SENSITIVE, MELODIC SECTIONS AS WELL AS ANTHEMIC, POWERFUL CHORUSES, LISTENERS CAN EXPECT TO HEAR ECHOES OF KARNIVOOL, VOLA, MASTODON, BLACK PEAKS, AND DIZZY MIZZ LIZZY. [cite: 3]

IN 2024, THE BAND SELF-RELEASED 3 SINGLES, EMPIRE, BLOOM, AND CIRCLES - TOUCHING ON THEMES OF RELIGION, EXCLUSION, AND GROWTH. [cite: 4]

NIRMATA WILL RELEASE THEIR DEBUT ALBUM AVERNO ON AUGUST 8, 2025 THROUGH LAST MILE RECORDS. [cite: 5]
